Nutrition all along science

Shrivatsa Chapalli
The meal plan is not only tailored just for packed protein,carbs and fats but also took many aspects into consideration.
The planned meals are made with proper nutritional values. we take some points for making proper foods which makes the digestion is easy. And we consider proper scale for acid/alkali and make the diet well balanced.
In case if you don't know about acidic/alkaline foods, our body always strives to maintain the ph value around 7.35-7.45, so the food containing a ph value of less than 7 are considered as acidic and more of 7 are alkaline.
The blood's ph value decreases when we consume acidic foods. but the body tries to neutralize the ph levels by consuming some minerals like calcium and get the ph values neutralised and get back on track. by this it weakens the bones.so we need to include more of alkaline foods in the diet
